Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about the platform

Do you charge platform fees?
No. We do not charge platform or transaction fees. We earn standard distributor commissions from AMCs on regular plans.
How are transactions executed?
All MF transactions are routed via BSE Star MF for reliability and compliance with industry standards.
Can NRIs invest?
Yes, subject to scheme eligibility and RBI/SEBI norms. We support NRE/NRO flows as per fund rules.
Is my data secure?
We follow strong encryption, OTP-based sign-in flows, and industry best practices for security and data protection.
